# Dashbox
A fast-paced endless runner game where you control a growing cube through procedurally generated obstacles. Double jump, collect coins, grow in size, and beat your high score!

Features:

🌈 Neon visual effects with animated UI

💰 Coin collection system (grow your player size)

⚡ Double jump mechanic

📊 Combo scoring system

🎵 Sound effects & background music

🏆 High score tracking

✨ Particle effects & dynamic backgrounds

Controls:

SPACE or Click = Jump

SPACE in mid-air = Double Jump

ESC = Quit
